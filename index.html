Shoot the Brussels Sprout

by: Amy Zhao

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I watched ads for a particular game and wanted to recreate it with different images.

2. What I Learned

I changed the movement of several objects in order to attain parabolic movement.

3. Architecture of My Game

The blaster releases bullets in order to demolish the brussels sprouts. The movement and images are controlled outside of the specific classes.

4. Technical Implementation

The parabolic movements of the brussels sprouts were done by adding a gravity variable, velocityX variable, and velocityY variable. By adding these factors, the brussels sprouts fall and bounce back in the shape of a parabola.

5. Most Challenging Part

The most challenging components of the project would be finding suitable images and making one image split into two.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

I would make the collision box less rectangular and more of an outline of the actual image
